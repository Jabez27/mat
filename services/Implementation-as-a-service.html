Frequently Asked Questions

Implementation as a Service is a model that offers businesses a streamlined and efficient way to implement new technologies or processes. This can include configuring, setting up, and integrating software and systems, as well as providing training and support for end-users.

Our team of experts has experience implementing a wide range of solutions, including enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems, customer relationship management (CRM) software, business intelligence and analytics tools, and more. We also offer customized solutions that are tailored to specific business needs and requirements.

The benefits of working with an Implementation as a Service provider include reduced risk, improved time-to-value, and lower costs. By outsourcing the implementation process to a team of experts, businesses can focus on their core operations while still getting the benefits of new technologies and processes.

Our Implementation as a Service process typically starts with an assessment of your current systems and processes, followed by the creation of a customized implementation plan. We then work with you to configure and set up your new solution, and provide training and support to ensure a smooth transition. Throughout the process, our team stays in close communication with you to ensure that your specific needs and requirements are being met.

Gap Analysis is a process used to identify the differences between a company's current state and its desired future state, specifically in the context of a technology implementation project. The goal of Gap Analysis is to identify areas for improvement and prioritize the implementation of new solutions to meet the desired future state.

MAKO team offers ongoing support and training during and after the implementation process to ensure a smooth transition and long-term success. This includes technical support, end-user training, and consultation services to help you get the most out of your new solution.

We use a proven project management methodology to ensure that implementations are completed on time and within budget. This includes regular progress updates, risk assessments, and proactive problem-solving to minimize any potential delays or issues.

Our Implementation as a Service process is designed to be highly flexible and customizable, so that we can align the implementation with your specific business goals and objectives. This includes regular check-ins to ensure that the implementation is progressing as planned, and that any changes or modifications are made in a timely and efficient manner.
